Why GIS

I chose to pursue a career in BGIS due to my interest in technology and data analytics and to be able to apply these tools in a variety of industries and fields. The BGIS program is a very hands-on and fast paced program that teaches GIS from the ground up and focuses on getting real-world experience of GIS through the structure of the courses and a capstone project with established companies. I would love to apply the skills I learn in the program like geospatial data management, data analytics and geosptail project workflows to real-world problems in the agriculture industry.
